Common Registration Issues

Quick answers to frequently asked registration questions.

I can't log in to the new portal

The new portal requires your byupathway.edu login, not your Church account. Check your email for a message titled "New BYU-Pathway Portal" with your new login credentials.

Steps to resolve:

  1. 1. Search your email for "New BYU-Pathway Portal"
  2. 2. Use the byupathway.edu login provided in that email
  3. 3. Sign in with your Church account when prompted
  4. 4. If you can't find the email, use login@byupathway.edu

My ecclesiastical endorsement is delayed

Endorsements can take time, especially if your leader is unfamiliar with the process. Here's how to help move things along:

What to do:

  • • Contact your bishop/branch president directly
  • • Provide them with your endorsement request details
  • • Ask them to check their email (including spam folder)
  • • Offer to help them access the endorsement system
  • • Consider asking a counselor to assist if needed

I'm getting a "hold" message when trying to register

Holds prevent registration until specific requirements are met. Common holds include:

Which path should I choose: PathwayConnect or Advanced?

PathwayConnect (Recommended)

  • • First three semesters include foundational courses
  • • No ACT/SAT required for admission
  • • All courses count toward your degree
  • • Built-in academic and religion courses
  • • Best for most students, especially first-time college

Advanced Path

  • • For students with significant university experience
  • • Primarily for U.S. students
  • • Requires 2.0+ GPA or 3.0 in PathwayConnect
  • • More detailed application process
  • • Direct entry to degree program
