Your Essential Student Tools

Understanding how all the systems connect will help you navigate your studies more effectively.

Student Portal

Your central dashboard for everything BYU Pathway. Access courses, grades, finances, personal information, and more.

  • View grades and transcripts
  • Manage finances and payments
  • Update personal information
  • Access student resources

Pro Tip

Check your Portal often for new "Holds", "Alerts" and school materials.

How to?

Canvas LMS

Your learning environment where you'll access course materials, submit assignments, and interact with instructors.

  • Read course materials and weekly modules
  • Complete assignments and quizzes
  • Participate in discussions
  • Communicate with instructors

Pro Tip

Download the "Canvas Student" app. It's a great way to get instant notifications for grades and messages.

How to?

Microsoft 365

Free access to professional productivity tools including Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and More.

  • Create documents and presentations
  • Collaborate with classmates
  • Store files in OneDrive
  • Access enterprise-grade productivity software free as a student

Pro Tip

Your school email (@byupathway.edu) gives you free access to tools like Microsoft 365 tools and more.

Microsoft 365 Setup Guide

Get free access to professional productivity tools that will help you succeed in your studies and career.

Step-by-Step Setup

1

Go to m365.cloud.microsoft

Use the direct portal link: m365.cloud.microsoft

2

Sign in with school email

Use your full @byupathway.edu email address

3

Authenticate account

Sign in with your Church Account credentials if prompted

4

Access your tools

Use M365 apps directly for your school work

You can download Office apps and sign in with your @byupathway.edu email for full offline access!
