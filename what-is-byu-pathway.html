How BYU-Pathway Works

Your journey from application to degree in clear, achievable steps.

1

Apply (It's Free!)

Submit your free application online. There's no external exams, or previous college experience required. BYU-Pathway is designed for everyone.

2

Start PathwayConnect

Begin with PathwayConnect — a one-year program that builds essential academic, professional, and spiritual skills while earning college credits.

3

Earn Certificates

Complete job-ready certificates in areas like Business, IT, Healthcare, and more. Each certificate can be finished in about one year.

4

Complete Your Degree

Stack three certificates to earn your Bachelor's degree — in as little as 3 years total. Graduate with real skills and zero campus visits required.

The Story Behind BYU-Pathway

From 50 students in 2009 to a global movement serving tens of thousands.

2009

Pathway Begins

BYU-Idaho launches Pathway with just 50 students in the United States.

2016

Rapid Growth

Pathway grows to serve 14,000+ students in over 60 countries.

2017

BYU-Pathway Worldwide Created

The First Presidency announces the creation of BYU-Pathway Worldwide as a separate organization.

Now

Global Impact

85,000+ students in 180+ countries with affordable, accessible education.
