Laptop Scholarships

Technology Grant

HSEF

Program Overview:

A non-profit dedicated to bridging the digital divide. They provide refurbished high-quality laptops to students in developing regions (Africa, Philippines, Pacific Islands), empowering them to pursue online education.

Requirements:

  • Have completed PC 101
  • Currently registered with BYU-Pathway
  • Looking for a job
  • Willing to attend the weekly career chats

How to Apply (Choose one):

  • Fill out this request form
  • Meet with your local ecclesiastical leader
  • Contact your BYU-Pathway mentor

Expect a response after your application is reviewed.

Equipment Aid

The Lord's Hands

Program Overview:

A charitable focus on lifting African students out of poverty. They grant free laptops to dedicated BYU-Pathway learners who lack the technology needed to complete their degree programs.

Requirements:

  • Must be a student in Africa
  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Competitive application via Student Dashboard

How to Apply:

  1. Visit site & create an account
  2. Fill out the form
  3. Important: Have your Student ID ready (learn to get your school ID)

Tuition Scholarships

Tuition Grant

Heber J. Grant (HJG)

Overview:

BYU-Pathway's primary financial aid program, designed to help students start debt-free. It provides tuition discounts (10-50%) to PathwayConnect and Online Degree students based on financial need.

Prerequisites:

PathwayConnect:

  • Register for courses
  • Complete self-reliance lesson in portal

Online Degree:

  • Apply at program start (reapply every 2 years)
  • Register at least 3 days before semester starts

How to Apply:

  1. Log in to your Student Portal
  2. Click on Finances menu
  3. Select Heber J. Grant Scholarship
  4. Complete the interactive lesson

External Foundation

Hall Foundation

Overview:

A philanthropic initiative by Brad Hall Companies to foster self-reliance. They offer supplementary tuition grants for students living outside the USA to help cover degree costs.

Prerequisites:

  • Must reside outside the United States
  • Completed PathwayConnect
  • Enrolled in an Online Degree program
  • Maintain a major GPA of 2.0 or higher

How to Apply:

  • Check website for open enrollment periods
  • Apply online via the portal (when open)
  • Submit required transcripts/documents
